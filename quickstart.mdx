---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get up and running with Deeto in minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to [app.deeto.ai](https://app.deeto.ai) and sign in with your company email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first advocates">
    Navigate to **Campaigns** and create a **New Advocates** campaign to invite your customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect content">
    Once advocates join, run an **Existing Advocates** campaign to collect testimonials, case studies, and reviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share references">
    Use **Microsites** to create personalized reference pages for your sales prospects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Referral Hub lets you create referral and affiliate programs where your customers or brand supporters submit potential leads and get rewarded for qualified referrals.

## Referral Hub tabs

### Programs

View and manage your referral programs. Each program card shows:

- Program name and description
- Status (online, offline, or archived)
- KPI metrics and conversion tracking
- Participant counts

**Program types:**

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Referral** | Standard referral program — customers refer prospects they know |
| **Affiliate** | Affiliate program — partners refer leads through trackable links |

### Leads

Track all referred prospects and their status through the qualification pipeline:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Lead just submitted |
| **Qualified** | Lead meets qualification criteria |
| **Won** | Lead converted to customer |
| **Closed** | Lead closed (not converted) |
| **Disqualified** | Lead didn't meet criteria |

### Referrers

Manage the people making referrals — view their profiles, referral performance metrics, and engagement history. You can also invite new affiliate partners from this tab.

## Creating a referral program

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Referral Hub">
    Navigate to **Workflows → Referral Hub → Programs**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new program">
    Click **Create referral program**. Set the program name, description, type (referral or affiliate), and eligibility criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure fields">
    Define which fields referrers and referees need to fill out. You can use standard fields or create custom fields per program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up rewards">
    Define what referrers receive when their leads reach specific statuses (qualified, won). See [Rewards](/rewards/overview) for reward configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design the landing page">
    Customize the look and feel of your referral landing page — background, images, logos, and colors to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Set the program to **online** and share the link with your customers and partners.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Self-registration pages

Self-registration pages are shareable links that let customers sign up as references without a direct invitation. To customize:

1. Find the shareable link for your program
2. Click **Edit look & feel**
3. Customize background, image, logo, and visual elements
4. Save and share the updated link

<Tip>Share self-registration links in newsletters, post-purchase emails, customer community channels, or your website to scale your reference pool.</Tip>

## Best practices

- **Keep submission forms short** — More fields = fewer completions
- **Communicate rewards clearly** — Referrers should know what they get and when
- **Follow up on leads quickly** — Qualify referred leads promptly to keep referrers engaged
- **Track ROI** — Monitor referral metrics in [Platform Performance](/admin/platform-performance) under the Leads tab

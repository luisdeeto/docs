This guide walks you through creating your first campaign in Deeto. If you haven't already, read the [Campaign types overview](/campaigns/overview) to understand which type fits your goal.

## Before you start

Make sure you have:

- Admin or Manager access to your Deeto workspace
- At least one reference or a list of contacts to invite
- A clear goal (recruit references, collect content, or broadcast a message)

## Campaign creation flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Campaigns">
    Go to **Workflows → Campaigns** and click **Create Campaign**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a campaign type">
    Select the type that matches your goal:

    | Goal | Campaign type |
    |------|-------------|
    | Recruit new references | **Reference Recruitment** |
    | Collect testimonials from existing references | **Content Generation** |
    | Send a one-time message | **Broadcasting to References** |
    | Conduct AI-powered interviews | **AI Interview Agent** |
    | Engage references in activities | **Activity Participation** |
    | Grow referral program | **Referral Growth** |
    | Create task assignments only | **Assignments Only** |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set campaign details">
    Fill in the **Details** step:
    - **Name** — A descriptive name (e.g., "Q2 Reference Recruitment" or "Post-Renewal Content Collection")
    - **Description** — What this campaign is for (internal reference)
    - **Schedule** — When to activate and optional deactivation conditions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your audience">
    In the **Audience** step:
    - **Upload contacts** — Add a CSV or select from existing references
    - **Segment** — Filter by industry, role, company size, or custom fields
    - **Dynamic audience** — Optionally connect via API for CRM-triggered enrollment (see [Dynamic audiences](/campaigns/dynamic-audiences))
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure messages">
    In the **Messages** step:
    - Write your email subject and body
    - Set up a multi-step sequence (space emails by days)
    - Use template variables for personalization (name, company, etc.)
    - Preview before saving
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and launch">
    Review all settings. You can save as **Draft** to launch later, or activate immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign statuses

After launching, your campaign moves through these stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Created but not yet launched |
| **Scheduled** | Set to launch at a future date |
| **Active** | Running — emails are going out |
| **Stopped** | Paused manually |
| **Ended** | Completion condition met automatically |
| **Archived** | Closed out |

## Recommended first campaigns

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="If you want quick wins">
    Start with **Reference Recruitment** targeting customers who gave high NPS scores. Use a short, friendly email sequence (3 emails over 2 weeks). Goal: get 10-20 new references in your pool.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="If you need content fast">
    Run a **Content Generation** campaign targeting existing references. Ask for quotes or short testimonials. Include a reward incentive. Goal: collect 15+ content assets in 3 weeks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="If you want to test AI interviews">
    Launch an **AI Interview** campaign with a small audience (10-15 references). Choose "First 30 days experience" or "Product feedback" as the topic. Review AI-generated summaries after a week.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for success

- **Keep email sequences to 3-4 emails** — More emails means more responses, but diminishing returns after 4
- **Send from a familiar name** — Use a real person's name, not "noreply@company.com"
- **Include a reward** — Even small incentives significantly boost participation
- **Set a completion condition** — Auto-end the campaign when you hit your target number

For setup instructions, see the [Widget Setup & Usage Guide](/publishing/website-widget). This page covers strategies for maximizing widget impact.

## 1. Build a solid content repository

Without a well-curated library of testimonials, the widget won't have the impactful content it needs. Here's how to build a strong foundation:

- **Collect diverse testimonials** — Reach out to customers across company sizes, industries, and use cases
- **Highlight specific benefits** — Encourage customers to share quantifiable results
- **Update regularly** — Keep content fresh with new testimonials and success stories

## 2. Match content to the page

Most companies still use dedicated testimonial pages — this isn't effective. Deeto's widget allows social proof throughout your website seamlessly.

| Page | Content strategy |
|------|-----------------|
| **Homepage** | Mix of high-level testimonials speaking to your overall value proposition |
| **Product pages** | Testimonials and case studies that specifically mention the product |
| **Feature pages** | Social proof that discusses the benefits of that specific feature |
| **Pricing page** | Customer testimonials about ROI and value to address price concerns |
| **Case studies page** | Link to full case studies from widget quotes for deeper exploration |

## 3. Mix it up and A/B test

Take advantage of the widget's flexibility:

- **Launch multiple variations** — Create at least two widget versions with different testimonials and place them on different pages
- **A/B test** — Compare performance of different content, layouts, and placements
- **Analyze and optimize** — Gather data on conversion rates and engagement for each variation

<Tip>Regularly mixing up your social proof content and testing different approaches ensures you are continuously optimizing for the best results.</Tip>

## Key takeaways

The goal is to build trust and credibility with your audience. Relevant, well-placed social proof is one of the most powerful tools to achieve that — but only if the content is fresh, the placement is strategic, and you're testing what works.

The Knowledge Hub is the heart of Deeto — a centralized repository where all customer voice and participation activities come together. It's organized into three tabs: **Content**, **People**, and **Companies**.

## Content (Assets)

Browse and manage all customer-generated content in one place.

**Content types stored:**

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Quotes** | Short customer endorsements |
| **Reviews** | Detailed customer reviews |
| **Case studies** | In-depth customer stories with rich formatting |
| **Video testimonials** | Video content from references |
| **Q&A** | Question-and-answer format content |
| **G2 reviews** | Reviews synced from [G2](/integrations/g2) |
| **Transcripts** | Call transcripts from [Gong](/integrations/gong) or AI interviews |
| **PDFs** | Uploaded documents |
| **URLs** | External links |
| **Win/Loss reports** | Analysis reports from win/loss campaigns |
| **Survey responses** | Structured survey data |

**View modes:** Switch between Dashboard, List, and Table views depending on how you prefer to browse.

**Filtering:** Filter content by type, industry, product, custom tags, sentiment, or date. Save complex filter combinations as **Saved Views** for quick reuse.

## People

Search and manage your references and customer contacts.

- View reference profiles with complete engagement history
- Filter by availability, engagement level, role, or company
- Track participation across campaigns, meetings, and content contributions
- See all content assets associated with each person

## Companies

Browse customer accounts at the organization level.

- View company profiles with associated references and content
- Filter by industry, company size, or engagement level
- Track company-level engagement metrics
- Identify high-value reference accounts

## How content gets into the Knowledge Hub

| Source | How it arrives |
|---|---|
| **Campaigns** | References submit content through [campaign](/campaigns/overview) contribution wizards |
| **Gong** | Call transcripts [sync automatically](/integrations/gong) daily |
| **G2** | Reviews [pull in automatically](/integrations/g2) via Auto-Sync |
| **CRM** | Contact and company data syncs from [Salesforce](/integrations/salesforce) or [HubSpot](/integrations/hubspot) |
| **Imported content** | Upload PDFs, URLs, and documents manually |
| **AI interviews** | Transcripts and summaries from [AI interview campaigns](/campaigns/overview#ai-interview) |

## Content approval workflow

When references submit content, it can go through an approval workflow before becoming available:

1. **Pending** — Content submitted, awaiting review
2. **Approved** — Content reviewed and approved for use
3. **Rejected** — Content doesn't meet quality standards

<Note>Configure approval settings in **Settings → Approval Settings** to control which content types require review before publishing.</Note>

## Best practices

1. **Make it a daily habit** — Check the Knowledge Hub before customer meetings, content creation, or strategic planning
2. **Use Saved Views** — Create views for common searches (e.g., "Enterprise quotes from last 90 days") so you don't have to rebuild filters each time
3. **Use the AI Assistant** — Ask natural language questions instead of browsing manually. See [AI Assistant best practices](/ai-assistant/best-practices)
4. **Keep profiles current** — Update customer records with role changes and new feedback

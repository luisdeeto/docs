  <Accordion title="What reward types are available?">
    Deeto supports three reward types:
    - **Gift cards** — Vendor-configured options (e.g., Amazon, Visa, Starbucks)
    - **Charitable donations** — Donate points to selected causes
    - **Custom rewards** — Vendor-specific options via custom URLs

    Available options are configured by your vendor/admin. Not all types may be enabled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do references earn points?">
    Points are awarded automatically when references complete qualifying activities:
    - Submitting content (quotes, reviews, testimonials, case studies, videos)
    - Having their content viewed by prospects
    - Completing reference calls or meetings
    - Participating in campaigns
    - Completing custom activities (webinars, product feedback, etc.)

    The number of points per activity is set by the admin in Settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum redemption amount?">
    Yes — your admin configures a minimum redemption value. Points are redeemed in fixed increments (e.g., $5 steps). The maximum per transaction is typically 1,000 points unless configured otherwise.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does fulfillment take?">
    Once a reference submits a redemption request:
    1. The request enters **Pending** status
    2. The admin or system processes the fulfillment
    3. The reward is delivered and status changes to **Fulfilled**

    Timing depends on the reward type and your organization's fulfillment process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the rewards after launching?">
    Yes. Admins can adjust point values per activity, add or remove gift card options, change minimum/maximum redemption amounts, and enable/disable donation options at any time. Changes apply to future earnings — already-earned points are not affected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a reference's content is rejected?">
    If submitted content is rejected during the approval process, the reward status is updated to **Revoked** and the points are removed from the reference's balance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can references see their reward history?">
    Yes — references see their available balance, pending redemptions, and fulfilled rewards in the **Rewards** section of their Deeto portal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track rewards spending as an admin?">
    Use [Platform Performance](/admin/platform-performance) → **Rewards** tab to see total points issued, redeemed, balances, popular reward types, and per-reference breakdowns.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are rewards visible to all references?">
    Rewards visibility is controlled by vendor settings. If `showAwards` or `showPromotionBannerPoints` is disabled, references won't see the Rewards section in their sidebar.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

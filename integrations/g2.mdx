The G2 Auto-Sync integration automatically pulls reviews from your G2 profile and syncs them into your Deeto Knowledge Hub. This gives you a unified repository of all customer voice — both first-party and third-party reviews.

## What you get

With G2 Auto-Sync enabled, Deeto will:

- Automatically import all reviews from your G2 profile
- Extract customer names, companies, and review text
- Tag reviews by industry, company size, and sentiment
- Create searchable customer profiles in your Knowledge Hub
- Keep reviews in sync as new ones are posted on G2
- Generate campaigns to request new G2 reviews from existing customers

## Prerequisites

- Active Deeto workspace
- G2 profile with existing reviews
- G2 account login credentials

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your G2 account">
    Log in to Deeto as an administrator. Navigate to **Settings → Integrations**. Find the **G2** tile and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Deeto">
    You'll be redirected to G2. Log in, review permissions, and click **Authorize**. You'll be redirected back to Deeto.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your G2 profile">
    If you manage multiple G2 profiles, select the one you want to sync. Confirm and click **Confirm and Sync**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for initial sync">
    The first sync may take a few minutes depending on your review volume. You'll see a progress indicator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure review campaigns (optional)">
    Navigate to **Campaigns**, create a **G2 Review Request** campaign, and target high-NPS or long-term customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How it works

**Initial sync** — Deeto pulls all existing G2 reviews, reviewer names, companies, review text, ratings, and dates.

**Ongoing sync** — After setup, Deeto checks for new reviews daily and adds them to your Knowledge Hub automatically.

**Search and use** — Once synced, you can search reviews in the Knowledge Hub, filter by industry/company size/rating, create microsites using G2 reviews, use reviews in your widget, and share quotes with your sales team.

## What reviews can be used for

| Team | Use cases |
|------|-----------|
| **Sales** | Battle cards with competitive proof, one-sheets, quotes for prospects |
| **Marketing** | Website widget display, case studies, social media testimonials |
| **Product** | Feature feedback, pain point identification, roadmap prioritization |
| **Customer Success** | Share positive reviews with accounts, track sentiment, find advocates |

## Best practices

- **Proactively request reviews** — Use Deeto campaigns to ask customers to review on G2
- **Timing matters** — Request reviews after successful implementations or positive interactions
- **Monitor sentiment** — Track how your reviews and ratings trend over time
- **Use in sales** — Share high-star reviews with prospects in late-stage deals

<Warning>Ensure any incentives for G2 reviews comply with G2's policies. Don't offer incentives based on review rating or content.</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Reviews not showing up | Verify the integration is active in Settings. Allow 24 hours for initial sync. |
| New reviews not syncing | Check your G2 profile is public and reviews are visible. Verify the integration is still active. |
| Can't authorize with G2 | Verify your G2 login credentials. Clear browser cache and cookies. |

## Managing your integration

- **Disconnect:** Settings → Integrations → G2 → Disconnect. Existing reviews remain in the Knowledge Hub.
- **Re-sync:** Settings → Integrations → G2 → Refresh Sync to pull any missed reviews.

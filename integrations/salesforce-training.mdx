This guide is for sales reps and AEs who use Deeto day-to-day within Salesforce. For admin setup instructions, see the [Salesforce Integration Setup](/integrations/salesforce).

## Why connect Deeto to Salesforce

Connecting Deeto to your CRM integrates references directly into your sales cycle. Your sales team gets access to customer proof without leaving Salesforce.

## Inviting a prospect to a reference experience

When your prospect asks for a reference (or you want to proactively offer one):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the opportunity">
    Open the **Opportunity** tab for the prospect you want to invite. If the prospect doesn't have a contact within the opportunity, create one first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Deeto and invite">
    Go to **Opportunity tab → Deeto tab → Invite Prospect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select reference matches">
    Choose the references you want to share. We recommend selecting **3 references** per prospect with a variety of assets from each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Personalize the experience">
    Add a personalized note per reference, confirm shared assets, and customize the email template. We recommend making it personal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send email**. The prospect receives a customized page with your selected references and their content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens next:**

1. Prospect chooses which reference(s) to connect with
2. Prospect suggests time slots for their chosen references
3. References receive the request with suggested time slots
4. Once agreed, calendar invites are sent to both parties
5. A reminder is sent before the call
6. You receive call details including notes via email notification

<Note>You can track everything in this process from your Deeto dashboards and from received notifications.</Note>

## Inviting a new reference

To invite a customer to become a reference:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the contact">
    Open the contact within Salesforce. If no contact exists, create one first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Deeto">
    Go to **Contact view → Deeto tab → Review → Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and send">
    Review the email template (check with your admin to ensure correctness), then click **Continue → Send**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices for AEs

### Working with prospects

- **Be proactive** — share relevant content ahead of the initial call to lead with authenticity
- **Define a "reference call" stage** in your sales process. Our top customers have a Deeto step that triggers reference calls at the right moment

### Recruiting references

- **Use NPS scores** — customers scoring 8+ are ideal reference candidates
- **Lead with their gain** — communicate what's in it for them (rewards, networking, early access)
- **Time it wisely** — best moments to invite are:
  - 60 days after onboarding (if it went smoothly)
  - During a QBR
  - Right after contract renewal
  - Consider adding a perk to the renewal process for becoming a reference

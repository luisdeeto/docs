This guide is for sales reps and AEs who use Deeto day-to-day within HubSpot. For admin setup instructions, see the [HubSpot Integration](/integrations/hubspot).

## What you see in HubSpot

After the integration is set up, you'll find Deeto embedded in your HubSpot records:

- **In Deals** — View recommended references, invite prospects to reference experiences, track reference call status
- **In Contacts** — See if a contact is a Deeto reference, their engagement history, and content they've contributed
- **In Companies** — View all references associated with the account and their participation history
- **Activity timeline** — Deeto events (reference calls, content submissions, prospect engagement) appear in HubSpot

## Inviting a prospect to a reference experience

When a prospect asks for a reference or you want to proactively offer one:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the deal in HubSpot">
    Navigate to the deal record for the prospect you want to connect with references.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Deeto tab">
    Click on the **Deeto** tab within the deal record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Prospect">
    Select **Invite Prospect** to start the matching process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select reference matches">
    Choose the references you want to share. We recommend selecting **3 references** per prospect with a variety of content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Personalize and send">
    Add a personalized note for each reference. Confirm shared assets. Customize the email template and click **Send**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens next:**

1. Prospect receives a personalized page with your selected references
2. Prospect chooses which reference(s) to connect with
3. Prospect suggests time slots
4. References receive the request and confirm times
5. Calendar invites are sent automatically
6. You receive call details and notes via email notification

## Viewing Deeto data in HubSpot

### In deal records

The Deeto tab in deals shows:
- **Recommended references** — Automatically matched based on prospect profile
- **Reference call status** — Scheduled, completed, or pending
- **Shared content** — Which assets were shared with the prospect
- **Engagement metrics** — How the prospect interacted with references

### In contact records

For contacts who are Deeto references:
- **Deeto ID** — Links the HubSpot contact to their Deeto profile
- **Contribution history** — Content they've submitted
- **Availability** — Whether they're available for calls

## How data syncs

| Direction | What syncs |
|-----------|-----------|
| **HubSpot → Deeto** | Contact details, company info, deal data, lifecycle events |
| **Deeto → HubSpot** | Deeto ID on contacts/companies/deals, ticket objects for reference activities |

Most syncs happen on demand (when you open the Deeto tab). A daily scheduled sync ensures completeness.

## Best practices

- **Be proactive** — Share relevant content ahead of calls, don't wait for prospects to ask
- **Check Deeto before calls** — Review available references and content for the prospect's industry
- **Use the activity timeline** — Deeto engagement events appear alongside your other HubSpot activities
- **Coordinate with CS** — If a reference is getting too many requests, coordinate through Deeto to prevent burnout

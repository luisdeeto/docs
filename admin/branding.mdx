Deeto allows you to customize branding across all customer-facing experiences — microsites, widgets, email templates, and internal interfaces.

## What you can brand

| Area | Where to find it | What you customize |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Platform & onboarding** | Profile image → Settings → Company profile | Company logo (top left corner + onboarding flow) |
| **Communications** | Settings → Email Templates | Email background, logo, button styling |
| **Microsites** | Microsites settings | Header background, typography, button colors |
| **Self-registration page** | Edit look & feel on the shareable link | Background, image, logo |
| **Referral program** | Referral Hub → Programs → New program | Full design hub flow |
| **Website widget** | Publishing → Website Widget → New Widget | Layout, colors, content selection |

## How to update your branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access branding settings">
    Log in as an administrator. Navigate to **Settings → Branding**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Click **Upload Logo**. Select your company logo file (PNG or SVG recommended). Position and resize as needed. Save changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize colors">
    Click on each color section (primary, secondary, accent). Use the color picker or enter hex codes. Preview changes in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update typography">
    Select your preferred font family. Adjust font sizes for different text elements. Set font weights for headings and body text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize email templates">
    Navigate to **Email Templates**. Edit headers and footers with your branding. Update colors, add contact details and social links. Preview the template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure microsite branding">
    Go to each microsite. Click **Branding Settings**. Apply your logo, colors, and typography. Save changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Branding elements

### Logo and colors

- **Primary Logo** — Displays on microsites, emails, and portal pages
- **Primary Color** — Used for buttons, links, and key UI elements
- **Secondary Color** — Supporting visual elements
- **Accent Color** — Highlights and call-to-action buttons

### Typography

- **Font Family** — Choose from system fonts or upload custom fonts
- **Font Sizing** — Control heading, body, and button text sizes
- **Font Weights** — Set bold, regular, and light variations

### Email templates

- **Email Header** — Your logo and custom messaging
- **Email Footer** — Company contact information and social links
- **Email Colors** — Match your brand color palette
- **Button Styling** — Customize call-to-action appearance

## Best practices

- **Be consistent** — Use the same logo, colors, and fonts across all Deeto experiences
- **Match your website** — Mirror your website branding exactly for a seamless experience
- **Ensure contrast** — Text should have sufficient contrast with backgrounds (WCAG 4.5:1 ratio for normal text)
- **Use high-resolution logos** — PNG or SVG format recommended
- **Limit to 2-3 colors** — Keep it clean and professional
- **Test on mobile** — Always verify branding on mobile devices

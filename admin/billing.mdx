Manage your Deeto subscription from **Settings → Billing**.

## Your plan

Your billing page shows:

- **Current plan** — Which Deeto tier you're on and included features
- **Usage** — Credit consumption, active seats, and feature usage
- **Billing cycle** — Next renewal date and billing frequency
- **Payment method** — Card or invoice details on file

## Understanding credits

Some Deeto features consume credits:

| Feature | Credit cost |
|---|---|
| AI Assistant queries | 2 credits per query |
| Content Studio assets | 40 credits per asset |
| AI Interview campaigns | Varies by interview length |

<Tip>Talk to your CSM about credit allocation if you're approaching your limit.</Tip>

## Optional add-on modules

Deeto offers optional modules beyond the core platform:

| Module | What it includes |
|---|---|
| **Go-to-Market Hub** | Reference matching, prospect microsites, scheduling, CRM integration |
| **Imported Assets** | Upload and manage existing case studies, PDFs, and documents |
| **Conversation Intelligence** | Gong integration for call transcript analysis |
| **G2 Auto-Sync** | Automated G2 review import and campaign management |

## Common requests

For any subscription changes, contact your CSM or email [support@deeto.ai](mailto:support@deeto.ai):

- **Add seats** — Additional user seats available at $100/seat/year
- **Upgrade plan** — Move to a higher tier with more features and credits
- **Add modules** — Enable optional add-ons
- **Update payment** — Change payment method or billing contact
- **Request invoices** — Access past invoices or request specific formats

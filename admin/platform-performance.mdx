The Platform Performance page gives you a comprehensive view of your reference program's health and impact. Access it via **Analytics → Platform Performance** in your Deeto workspace.

## Tab-by-tab overview

### References

Shows the overall health of your reference base — total invited vs. joined, growth over time, breakdown by status/role, and contribution levels.

**Use it to:** Monitor pool growth, identify coverage gaps by industry or role, spot inactive references that need re-engagement.

### Content

Overview of all customer-generated content — quotes, reviews, video testimonials, case studies, and Q&As. Shows content types, status, contributors, and performance.

**Use it to:** Ensure diverse content types, identify prolific contributors, prioritize high-engagement content.

### Activities

Timeline of all reference engagement — invitations, calls, content submissions, campaign participations, reward redemptions.

**Use it to:** See real-time engagement, identify participation patterns, spot early warning signs.

### Rewards

Full picture of your rewards program — total points issued/redeemed, balances by reference, popular reward types, spending by activity.

**Use it to:** Budget for fulfillment, see which rewards are most popular, identify high-value contributors.

### Leads

Leads generated through your reference program — from referral links, microsite activity, lead quality and conversion, attributed revenue.

**Use it to:** Measure ROI, identify which references generate the highest-quality leads.

### Campaign Recipients

Participation in each outreach campaign — per-campaign recipient status, completion rates, email engagement.

**Use it to:** Monitor campaign health, identify unresponsive segments, optimize email content.

### Email Activities

Performance of all emails sent through Deeto — send volume, open rates, click-through rates, delivery status.

**Use it to:** Identify high-performing subject lines, spot deliverability issues, optimize timing.

### Account Level — Contributions

Reference activity aggregated at the company level — most active accounts, content per account, engagement level.

**Use it to:** Identify most engaged customers, plan account expansion, ensure balanced representation.

### Prospect Engagements

Every interaction between prospects and references — matching history, calls, content shared, engagement outcomes, revenue impact.

**Use it to:** Measure how references influence deal closure, identify effective references for specific prospect types.

### Prospect Microsite Activities

Prospect behavior on personalized microsites — visits, time spent, content clicked, form submissions, call scheduling, sharing.

**Use it to:** Identify hot prospects, see which content resonates, prioritize outreach.

### Potential References

Customers showing positive sentiment in external sources (Gong calls, G2 reviews) who haven't joined the program yet.

**Use it to:** Proactively recruit new references based on positive signals.

## Using Platform Performance for strategy

| Review cadence | What to check |
|---------------|--------------|
| **Monthly** | Reference growth trend, new content submissions, email campaign performance, reward spending vs. budget |
| **Quarterly** | Overall program health, prospect engagement and deal impact, revenue attribution, content gaps |
| **Annually** | Year-over-year comparison, full ROI analysis, program maturity assessment, strategic planning |

## Best practices

1. **Review regularly** — Set a calendar reminder to check weekly or monthly
2. **Track trends** — Look at changes over time, not just snapshots
3. **Share with stakeholders** — Use the data to justify program investment
4. **Act on insights** — Use low engagement data to identify issues; double down on what works
5. **Benchmark** — Track metrics month-over-month and year-over-year

This guide walks you through configuring SAML 2.0 single sign-on between Deeto and Okta.

<Note>Only existing users on Deeto will be authenticated via SAML. Deeto does not create vendor users when coming from SAML.</Note>

## Prerequisites

- Okta admin dashboard access
- Entity ID from Deeto (a GUID provided by your Deeto CSM)

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Okta admin">
    Sign in to your Okta admin dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create app integration">
    Go to **Menu → Applications → Applications**. Click **Create App Integration → SAML 2.0** and click "Next". Enter the app name: **Deeto**. Optionally upload the Deeto icon. Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure SAML details">
    On the **Configure SAML** details page, under **General**:
    - **Single sign on URL:** `https://api.deeto.ai/v1/saml/{entityID}` (replace `{entityID}` with the GUID from Deeto)
    - **Audience URI:** Same entity ID
    - **Name ID format:** EmailAddress
    - Leave everything else in General unchanged
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add attribute statements">
    In the **Attribute Statements** section, add these 3 attributes:

    | Name | Format | Value |
    |------|--------|-------|
    | `email` (lowercase) | Unspecified | `user.email` |
    | `firstName` (lowercase f, capital N) | Unspecified | `user.firstName` |
    | `lastName` (lowercase l, capital N) | Unspecified | `user.lastName` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and continue">
    Click **Preview the SAML assertion** to verify. Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete feedback page">
    On the Feedback page, select "This is an internal app that we have created". Click **Finish**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign users">
    Under the Deeto application page, go to the **Assignments** tab. Choose the users that will have access to the Deeto application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share metadata with Deeto">
    Click on **View SAML Setup instructions** under the "About" section. Find the section that says "IDP metadata to your SP Provider". Copy the whole text from the textbox and share it with Deeto.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Testing

After setup, go to your application board in Okta and click on **Deeto** to test the SSO flow.

<Warning>Only existing Deeto users will be authenticated. If a user doesn't exist in Deeto, SAML login will not create them automatically.</Warning>

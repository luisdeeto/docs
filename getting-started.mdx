Deeto is the customer voice and reference management platform that helps you capture, organize, and activate authentic customer stories at scale.

With Deeto, you can:

- **Collect customer intelligence** through surveys, interviews, and feedback
- **Organize and search** all customer feedback in a centralized Knowledge Hub
- **Deploy campaigns** to invite references, collect content, and gather feedback
- **Create personalized microsites** showcasing customer proof to prospects
- **Measure impact** with reference call booking and engagement analytics

## Your first week checklist

- [ ] Confirm your Deeto workspace is activated
- [ ] Invite your team members (at least 2-3 key users)
- [ ] Connect your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot) if applicable
- [ ] Connect your data sources (Gong, G2, surveys, etc.)
- [ ] Run your first campaign or query
- [ ] Attend team onboarding session

## Key features overview

### Knowledge Hub

Your centralized repository for all customer voice. Stores feedback, quotes, case studies, and contributor information. Search by customer name, sentiment, topic, or date.

### Campaigns Hub

Design and deploy campaigns to:

- Invite new references through onboarding campaigns
- Collect specific content (quotes, videos, case studies)
- Conduct AI-powered interviews for win-loss analysis, churn studies, or product feedback
- Trigger campaigns automatically from your CRM based on lifecycle events

### Go-to-Market Hub

Connect customer proof to revenue opportunities:

- Manage your reference pool
- Get automatic matching between prospects and references
- Deploy personalized Microsites to prospects
- Track reference call bookings and engagement

### AI Assistant

Ask natural language questions about your customer data:

- "What are customers saying about our onboarding?"
- "Find enterprise customers who mentioned ROI"
- "Summarize feedback from the last quarter"

## Getting your data into Deeto

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Deeto integrates with [Salesforce](/integrations/salesforce) and [HubSpot](/integrations/hubspot). This enables automatic reference matching, campaign triggering, and insight surfacing. Your Deeto CSM can help configure field mappings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect existing data sources">
    - **[Gong](/integrations/gong):** Automatically import call transcripts and sentiment
    - **[G2](/integrations/g2):** Sync reviews and set up review generation campaigns
    - **Surveys:** Import existing survey responses
    - **Imported Content:** Upload case studies, testimonials, or other documents
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch your first campaign">
    Choose one based on your immediate needs:
    - **Win/Loss Analysis** — Understand why deals are won or lost
    - **Product Feedback** — Gather customer input on features and improvements
    - **Reference Recruitment** — Invite customers to become active references
    - **Content Collection** — Request quotes, videos, or case studies
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adoption best practices

### Week 1-2: Foundation

- Ensure at least 3 team members have access and have logged in
- Run one test campaign or query
- Identify one stakeholder who will champion Deeto adoption

### Week 3-4: Integration

- Schedule one team demo showing how Deeto integrates with existing workflows
- Define 1-2 use cases where Deeto will be used regularly
- Set up one CRM integration or data source connection

### Month 2+: Embedding

- Deeto becomes part of regular workflows (pre-call research, deal support, content creation)
- Team proactively asks for customer intelligence
- Quarterly business reviews with insights from Deeto data

## Common first steps by role

| Role | Recommended first steps |
|------|------------------------|
| **Sales Leaders & AEs** | Connect CRM, explore the reference pool, schedule a reference call |
| **Product & PMM** | Run a win/loss campaign, use AI Assistant for feature feedback |
| **Customer Success** | Search Knowledge Hub for account-specific feedback, run expansion campaigns |
| **Marketing** | Import existing case studies, run G2 review campaigns, create content assets |

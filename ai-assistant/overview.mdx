The AI Assistant lets you query all your customer voice data using natural language. Instead of building complex searches and filters, just ask a question and get an answer drawn from your entire [Knowledge Hub](/knowledge-hub/overview).

## How to access it

The AI Assistant appears as a **sidebar panel** on the right side of your Deeto workspace. It's available on most pages:

- Dashboard
- Prospect Hub
- Knowledge Hub
- Campaigns
- Research Hub

Click the AI chat icon to open it, or collapse it when you don't need it.

## What you can do

### Ask questions about your data

- "What are customers saying about our onboarding experience?"
- "Find enterprise customers who mentioned ROI"
- "Which accounts expressed frustration about support response times?"

### Request summaries

- "Summarize all feedback from [Account Name] in the last 6 months"
- "What were the top 3 themes from last quarter's interviews?"
- "How has sentiment about our API changed over the last year?"

### Find specific content

- "Find positive quotes about [feature] from enterprise customers"
- "Show me customer stories from healthcare companies"
- "Find 3 strong quotes about our competitive advantages"

### Create content

- "Draft a case study summary based on [Account Name]'s feedback"
- "Create a battle card with competitive proof points"
- "Generate a one-sheet showing customer satisfaction metrics"

## Key features

- **Conversation threading** — Multi-turn conversations that build on context
- **Chat history** — Access previous conversations and continue where you left off
- **Context awareness** — The AI knows which page you're on and adjusts suggestions accordingly
- **Draft management** — Save messages before sending them
- **Cross-reference data** — Queries span all data sources: interviews, surveys, reviews, CRM data, call transcripts

## Credits

AI Assistant queries consume **2 credits per query**. Content Studio assets consume **40 credits per asset**. Monitor your credit usage in [Billing](/admin/billing).
